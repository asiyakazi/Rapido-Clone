App Safety Features

Trip Sharing

Riders can share their trip status with their respective emergency contacts on the Rapido app right after their ride starts.

Information Masking

Every woman rider's contact number is masked to protect their privacy and their interaction with the Captain happens only through app encryption.

SOS Button

The app has an SOS button which is a 24*7 in-app support feature.

Captain Information

Captain's name, vehicle number, model name and ratings are accessible to riders.

On Ground Support

Dedicated support teams help riders and captains whenever assistance is required.

Way forward on Safety

To further substantiate our commitment to Safety First towards customers & captains, we will be incorporating new features on our platform very soon.

Know More ↓

Ride Completion Check

Anyone who takes a ride between 10 pm and 6 am will be contacted by Rapido Customer Care for a safety check after the ride is completed.

Proactive Check-In

Every rider will be alerted on the system and called by Rapido Customer Care when there is any unusual activity or deviation from the set protocol.
