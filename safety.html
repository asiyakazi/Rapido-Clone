At Rapido, the well-being of our customers is above everything else. We are constantly in pursuit of enhancing our safety measures to ensure every Rapido ride is a pleasant and comfortable experience.

Measures to ensure the well-being of both, our Captains and Customers.

Insurance

Insurance can be claimed for any accident that occurs during the ride covering OPD treatment, hospitalisation and accidental benefit with a maximum sum insured of ₹5 Lakh.

24 × 7 Customer Support

Both, our captains and customers can report any kind of issues through the 24×7 support feature on the app post & during the ride.

Two-way Rating System

Post the ride, both parties can give a rating to each other and any rating below 3 is flagged by Rapido.

Way forward on Safety

To further substantiate our commitment to Safety First towards customers & captains, we will be incorporating new features on our platform very soon.

Ride Completion Check

Anyone who takes a ride between 10 pm and 6 am will be contacted by Rapido Customer Care for a safety check after the ride is completed.

Proactive Check-In

Every rider will be alerted on the system and called by Rapido Customer Care when there is any unusual activity or deviation from the set protocol.
